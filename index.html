About The Awards

The CSI Legacy Awards stand as a prestigious celebration of excellence in Corporate Social Investment (CSI) in South Africa. This annual event honours the remarkable efforts of organisations and individuals who have made significant contributions to addressing the country’s pressing challenges.

The judging process is a cornerstone of the Awards’ integrity. Submissions are anonymised to ensure unbiased evaluations, with judges working independently from various locations across the country. This decentralised approach guarantees impartiality and enables a fair assessment.
